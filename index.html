About Us

Our company, G.D. Tan & Co., Inc., is one of the oldest local stock brokerage houses in the Philippines, having been a member of the Manila Stock Exchange since our establishment in 1968. We have been ranked among the Top 10 Companies among local stock brokers prior to the Exchange's unification with the Makati Stock Exchange, in forming the Philippine Stock Exchange.

Our Principles

We commit ourselves to providing our clients with the best possible service in terms of market advice and research.

Our firm is committed to global competitiveness, offering traditional and innovative products of services that cater to the needs of corporate, middle market, and retail clients.

Diligence, dedication, and determination--

We act as our clients' agent in executing orders, taking care that their instructions are carried out efficiently with strict adherence to ethical standards. Our diligence in maintaining the performance of the firm's stock pickings and full disclosure to clients of the firm's preferences also ensure maximization of investment risks. Our firm is also a licensed dealer in securities, a function it likewise effectively carries out through participation in Initial Public Offerings (IPO) of stocks.

Our substantial placements and deposits in reputable banks also make our company financially sound as far as stock brokering and dealership are concerned. This is because as a company, we believe in offering our clients security with customer focus, innovation, professional expertise, and quality service.

These are the key principles that have helped shape G.D. Tan & Co., Inc. for more than 40 years. Yes, these are the same ideals that helped shape G.D. Tan & Co., Inc. to maintain its formidable standing in the equity market, which is now experiencing incredible globalization and liberalization.
